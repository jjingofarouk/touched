Get Involved

Join us in making a lasting impact on communities in Uganda through volunteering, donating, fundraising, or partnering with Touched Hearts.

Why Get Involved?

At Touched Hearts, we believe that every individual has the power to create change. Whether you're passionate about education, healthcare, disability support, or community development, your involvement helps us reach more people and transform lives. Together, we can build a future filled with hope, compassion, and opportunity.

Explore the many ways you can contribute below and become part of our mission to empower communities across Uganda.

Volunteer with Us

Volunteering with Touched Hearts is a rewarding way to make a direct impact. Whether you're teaching children, assisting with healthcare initiatives, or supporting community projects, your time and skills can change lives.

Opportunities Include:

  • Education Support: Tutor students or assist with school supply drives.
  • Healthcare Outreach: Help with mobile clinics or health education workshops.
  • Community Building: Participate in construction projects or skill-building programs.
  • Event Support: Organize or assist at fundraising and awareness events.

No matter your background, we welcome your enthusiasm and dedication. Fill out the form below to join our volunteer team!

Make a Donation

Your financial support fuels our programs and allows us to reach more individuals in need. Every dollar counts—whether it's funding school supplies, medical care, or community infrastructure, your donation creates a ripple effect of positive change.

How Your Donation Helps:

  • $50: Provides textbooks for 10 students.
  • $100: Funds a week of healthcare supplies for a rural clinic.
  • $200: Supports a month-long community training program.

Ready to contribute? Click below to make a secure donation and see the difference you can make.

Start a Fundraiser

Turn your passion into action by organizing a fundraising campaign for Touched Hearts. Whether it’s a birthday fundraiser, a charity run, or a community event, your efforts can raise vital funds and awareness for our cause.

Steps to Get Started:

  1. Plan Your Campaign: Choose an event or activity that excites you.
  2. Download Our Guide: Get tips and resources to make your fundraiser a success.
  3. Spread the Word: Use social media and our toolkit to rally support.
  4. Collect Donations: Direct funds to our secure donation page.

Download our Fundraising Guide to kickstart your journey and inspire others to join the movement!

Become a Partner

We collaborate with businesses, organizations, and institutions to amplify our impact. Partnerships allow us to pool resources, share expertise, and reach more communities effectively.

Partnership Benefits:

  • Corporate Social Responsibility: Enhance your brand by supporting a meaningful cause.
  • Joint Projects: Co-create initiatives tailored to your goals and ours.
  • Visibility: Gain recognition on our website, events, and reports.
