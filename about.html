About Touched Hearts

Our Story

Touched Hearts was founded in 2010 with a mission to support Uganda’s most vulnerable communities, including children, women, and persons with disabilities. Our journey began with a small group of passionate volunteers dedicated to making a difference. Today, we have impacted thousands through education, healthcare, and empowerment programs.

Our Mission

We strive to uplift underprivileged individuals by providing access to basic needs, education, and sustainable development opportunities.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every child and vulnerable person in Uganda has access to healthcare, education, and a dignified life.

Why We Exist

Education for All

We provide scholarships and learning materials to children from low-income families.

Healthcare Support

We offer free medical check-ups and ensure access to essential health services.

Women’s Empowerment

We equip women with vocational skills to help them achieve financial independence.
