Education Initiatives

Empowering Uganda’s future through transformative education

Our Commitment to Education

At Touched Hearts, we believe education is the cornerstone of a thriving community. Since 2010, we’ve worked tirelessly to break down barriers to learning for Uganda’s most vulnerable children. From providing scholarships to building schools, our initiatives aim to ensure every child has access to quality education, equipping them with the skills and knowledge to build a brighter future.

What We Do

Scholarships Program

We offer scholarships to children from low-income families, covering tuition, uniforms, and learning materials. In 2024 alone, we supported over 5,000 students across Uganda, ensuring financial barriers don’t stand in the way of their education.

School Infrastructure

We build and renovate schools in rural areas, creating safe, conducive learning environments. Our projects include classrooms, libraries, and sanitation facilities, benefiting thousands of students and teachers annually.

Teacher Training

We empower educators through professional development workshops, enhancing teaching quality. Topics include modern pedagogy, inclusive education, and child psychology, impacting over 1,000 teachers since inception.

Adult Literacy Programs

We extend education to adults, offering literacy and numeracy classes to parents and community members. This strengthens family support for children’s learning and fosters community development.
