Latest News & Updates

Stay updated with our latest initiatives and community efforts.

Scholastic Materials Given to School Children

February 20, 2025

During our recent community outreach, we provided essential school supplies to hundreds of children. Many families in underserved areas struggle to afford basic learning materials, affecting children's education.

Through generous donations, we distributed books, pencils, school bags, and uniforms to support their education and encourage school attendance. Parents expressed gratitude, noting how these materials will ease the financial burden.

Medical Camp Brings Healthcare to Karankoja

February 10, 2025

Our recent medical camp in Karankoja provided free medical services to hundreds of people who lack access to quality healthcare.

A dedicated team of doctors, nurses, and volunteers offered general check-ups, maternal care, immunizations, and health education. Many patients, including the elderly and children, received the treatment they urgently needed.

We plan to hold more medical camps in remote regions to ensure healthcare reaches those who need it the most.

Empowering Women Through Skills & Tools

January 28, 2025

Our community skilling program provided training and tools to widows, young mothers, and marginalized groups, helping them build sustainable livelihoods.

Participants were trained in tailoring, handicrafts, and entrepreneurship, followed by the distribution of sewing machines and other essential tools.

This initiative aims to create economic opportunities and financial independence for vulnerable women, empowering them to provide for their families.
